Steps to Explore Careers

  1. Self-Assessment: Reflect on your interests, values, skills, and personality to identify potential career paths that align with who you are.
  2. Research Industries and Roles: Use online resources, industry reports, and company websites to learn about different fields and job functions.
  3. Informational Interviews: Reach out to professionals in your areas of interest to gain insights into their careers and the industry.
  4. Networking: Attend industry events, join professional organizations, and connect with alumni to expand your network and learn about opportunities.
  5. Gain Experience: Pursue internships, volunteer work, or part-time jobs to get hands-on experience and a better understanding of various roles.
